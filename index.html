The main problems in learning

Neither at school nor at the institute do they teach us how to do the right thing study the material. We are preparing for exams and learning tickets. We train to solve monotonous tasks in order to it is better to pass the test, but often in the end it does not give us real knowledge. Cramming fades quickly and does not brings benefits.

Conclusion: you also need to be able to study, but for some reason this is not taught anywhere. What to do about it?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, make it effective and exciting. The same techniques are used on the example of learning in Practicum.

Teaching techniques

Five practices from Barbara Oakley

  • Two types of attention

    Deep meanings arise if you alternate focused and scattered thinking.

  • Recall

    Remember what you have learned - this will allow you to connect disparate portions of memory.

  • Interleaving

    Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

  • Questions

    Listening to the teacher, come up with a good question that really worries you.

  • The illusion of competence

    Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it just seems to you that you own a new topic.

The story of Barbara Oakley.

Since childhood, Barbara has not been given mathematics. She considered herself a complete humanitarian, and far from the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to get a raise, so successfully that she was promoted to commander. But in order to be promoted, you had to take math. And then Barbara came up with her own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out that if something is "poorly given" to you, your hard-won knowledge is much deeper than that of those to whom everything is clear at first glance.
